
	•	Start by talking and getting to know them.
	•	Ask them if they are hungry.
	•	Ask them if they want to bathe.
	•	Ask if they have somewhere to stay the night.
	•	Ask if they have a change of clothes.
	•	Ask if they would like a job at Speak Up. If yes, give them information or a “Job Opportunity” card.
	•	Ask if they need to see a doctor to get checked out.
	•	Make sure they have a few dollars so they can maybe eat later.
